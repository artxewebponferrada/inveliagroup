About us

Invelia Group is a company that, through its subsidiary EXPANDPERSISTENCE, LDA, specializes in energy consulting focused on hydrocarbons and their derivatives.

Presentation Contact

Who are we?

We provide representation services for buying and selling companies, sector marketing, advisory and geopolitical consultancy in the field of oil, oil products and gas markets. We also facilitate the acquisition of hydrocarbons and their by-products.

Our mission

In the competitive hydrocarbon sector, companies are constantly looking for ways to optimize their operations and maximize the value of their assets. As specialized oil, gas and derivatives consultants, our firm plays a crucial role in providing experience and expertise that enable our clients to navigate the complex challenges of the global hydrocarbon market. Using best practices and proven solutions, we help our clients make more strategic purchases and sales, ensuring that each transaction is conducted safely and profitably.
