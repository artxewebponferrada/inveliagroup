Engineering that transforms territories

At Invelia Group we have more than 25 years of experience in public and civil works, providing solutions in building, metallurgy, roadworks, and applied engineering.

Presentation Contact

Construction, Civil Works and Public Works

Our trajectory is marked by projects that have improved Spain’s social and economic connectivity. We understand the sector’s needs and respond with efficiency, innovation, and commitment. Technical specialization, strategic vision, and nationwide presence define our way of executing and supporting each client.

Earthworks

We master all phases of land conditioning, ensuring efficiency, safety, and adaptation to design: excavation and emptying, fillings and embankments, leveling and grading, slope execution, as well as specific operations for footings, braces, wells, and trenches.

Building

We offer a global proposal based on quality and active listening. We execute formwork and structures, masonry, plumbing and air conditioning, electrical installations, carpentry (metal, wood, PVC) and ornamental roofing for housing, hotels, health centers and residences, cultural and sports facilities, educational centers, and industrial warehouses.

Metallurgical Excellence

Our qualified human capital and advanced mechanical resources allow us to undertake industrial installations with maximum guarantees: refrigeration plants (Freon, NH₃, CO₂, glycol brines) with special steels for demanding thermal ranges, and metallic installations in carbon steel, galvanized steel, and stainless steel, with insulation, tube coils, and high-precision welding.

Cleaning and Maintenance

Specialized services for offices, residences, museums and cultural spaces, sports facilities, educational and health centers. Tailored and efficient maintenance with sustainability criteria, responsible products, and promotion of labor inclusion.

Road Maintenance

Strategic ally for concessionaire companies: technical inspections, cleaning of drains and signs, sealing of cracks and fissures, clearing, masonry and gardening works, and rehabilitation of structural elements, with versatile teams and experienced technical staff.

Civil and Industrial Engineering

Multidisciplinary team with expertise in structural calculation and carbon footprint assessment in construction projects. We develop technical and architectural design, drafting of projects, and management of civil works, building, and industrial engineering, combining local knowledge with international standards.
